Samantha Lee Counseling

MA, LMHCA – Clinical Associate - Seattle, WA
Licensed Mental Health Counselor Associate

I am a licensed mental health counselor associate who specializes in working with children (age 5+), adolescents, and parents. I believe both prevention and early intervention are essential when working with a young population and has a deep appreciation for the role parents have on the well-being of their children

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology from Pepperdine University and a Master’s degree in Applied Child and Adolescent Psychology from the University of Washington. I received clinical training at Seattle Children’s Autism Center where I provided RUBI Parent Training for Noncompliance and Disruptive Behaviors, as well as direct therapy services to children using the framework of Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA).

I endorse the use of evidence-based interventions yet acknowledges that there is not a one-size-fits-all approach for every child and parent in need of support. To meet the needs of my clients, I view the counseling process as a collaborative effort, incorporating personal considerations into therapy.
